Some knowledge on keyboards

Apple Keyboard

The Apple Keyboard offers a sleek, minimalist design with responsive keys and an improved typing experience.

English Typing Keyboard

This layout is perfect for fast and accurate typing, with keys arranged in the standard QWERTY format.

Mechanical Keyboard

Mechanical keyboards are known for their durability and tactile feedback, preferred by gamers and typists.

Ergonomic Keyboard

Ergonomic keyboards are designed to reduce strain on the hands and wrists, promoting healthier typing posture.

Wireless Keyboard

Wireless keyboards offer the convenience of cable-free use, perfect for a clean and clutter-free workspace.
