Typing Learning Page

Rules for Typing:

Place your fingers on the keyboard according to the finger placement table below.
Press the correct keys displayed in each row.
If you make a mistake, the text will be highlighted in red.
Click the Retry button to practice a row again.
Focus on accuracy first, then speed.
Use the correct fingers for each key.
Keep your eyes on the screen, not on the keyboard.
Take regular breaks to avoid strain and fatigue.
Maintain proper posture while typing to prevent injuries.
Celebrate small wins! Keep improving with each practice session.
Practice regularly to see steady progress.
